2.3 Background

BEAD is an online study to better understand the asssociations between interoception, affective processing, and earlier life experiences (such as adversity, puberty, and trauma). It also includes outcome measures such as current depressive and anxious symptoms. Data was collected online from UCLA students using SONA.
